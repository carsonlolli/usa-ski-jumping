Our Mission

BORN TO
FLY HIGH

USA Ski Jumping funds programs from grassroots junior camps all the way to the teams competing at the 2026 Winter Olympics in Milano Cortina, creating accessible pathways for athletes across the country.

501(c)(3) Nonprofit · Tax-Deductible
🏆
Elite Competition
Supporting athletes at the highest levels — World Cup, Four Hills Tournament, and the 2026 Olympic Winter Games.
🎯
Youth Development
Our national development pipeline identifies and nurtures talent from junior levels upward, building the next generation of American ski jumpers.
🌍
Global Representation
Representing American athletes on the world stage — from Continental Cup circuits to Olympic podiums.
