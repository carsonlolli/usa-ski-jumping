Unlike many countries where ski jumping is fully government-funded, American athletes rely on supporters like you to train, travel, and compete on the world stage. Choose a sponsorship tier below and directly fund an athlete's Olympic dream.

Why It Matters

🎿
Training & Coaching
Covers world-class coaching, strength and conditioning programs, and year-round training at elite facilities across the U.S. and Europe.
✈️
Travel & Competition
Funds travel to World Cup events, Continental Cup circuits, and the 2026 Winter Olympics in Milano Cortina, Italy.

Equipment & Support
Provides high-performance suits, skis, helmets, and essential gear, plus sports science and recovery support.
